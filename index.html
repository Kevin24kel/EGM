Our Machine

  • Eco Solvent DX5 Machine

    Eco-solvent ink is a type of printing ink that is designed to be more environmentally friendly than traditional solvent-based inks. It uses milder, biodegradable solvents, resulting in lower VOC (volatile organic compound) emissions and a reduced environmental impact. Eco-solvent inks are commonly used in large-format printing for signage, vehicle wraps, and banners.

  • T-Shirt Press Machine

    A heat press machine is a specialized device used to apply heat and pressure to transfer designs onto various materials, most commonly fabrics like t-shirts.

  • Mug Press Machine

    A mug press machine is a specialized heat press designed to transfer designs onto mugs, tumblers, and other cylindrical drinkware.

  • Lanyard Press Machine

    A lanyard press machine is a type of heat press specifically designed for imprinting designs onto lanyards using heat transfer techniques.

  • Thumbler Press Machine

    A tumbler press machine, also known as a tumbler heat press, is a specialized tool designed for applying heat and pressure to transfer designs onto drinkware like tumblers, mugs, and bottles using sublimation printing.

  • Cutter Plotter Press Machine

    A cutter plotter press machine, often shortened to cutter plotter, is a computer-controlled device used to precisely cut various materials based on digital designs.
